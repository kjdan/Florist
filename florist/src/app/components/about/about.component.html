About Us

At The Flower Tale, we specialize in creating simple and classic bouquets for various occasions. We aim to make each arrangement both interesting and elegant, ensuring a perfect balance in design. Every bouquet is crafted with attention to detail and a passion for florals, bringing a touch of nature’s beauty to your special moments.

Ann is the head florist with a love for classic floral styles, focusing on timeless elegance and refined arrangements.

Liv is an expert in colors and combinations, crafting balanced and appealing arrangements.

Emma specializes in arranging flowers and using them to enhance decor, ensuring each setting is beautifully styled.
